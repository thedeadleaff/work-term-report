Work Term 2 | W22

Scotiabank - Fraud Management

Cybersecurity, criminal investigations and law have always peaked my interest. In this role I was able to work with data for real world, real time cases which required an eye for detail, good communication skills, and creativity. I used a variety of tools throughout my work term, but also utilized the downtime in my schedule to expand my technical learning. Python, Web Design, PowerShell and a look into microcomputing were all tapped into and have helped me through this role, and in my learning and growth overall.


Goals

Throughout this work term, I had main goals that spanned outside of this role. With this, it was difficult to dial in, and curate a path I wished to take despite my habit of always looking too far ahead.


  • I wish to be able to manage my work well and delivery quality solutions for tasks
  • I wish to be able to gain insight on work with the resources availible without feeling the stress of reading for prolonged periods of time
  • To integrate as much detail into my work; enrich the experience of readers who will be working with documents I will be assisting in completing

My above goals were not only met, but the learning that I have gained has helped direct my in a path that will best suit my strengths, and offer chances for me to improve on my weaknesses.

Goal number 1, a creative thinking goal, was set to ensure that I was able to identify if I was falling short, and how I can gain knowledge to help me complete tasks with great detail and efficiency. I used my creativity, and gathered as much information as I could to offer as much context possible for other employees on teams, and readers after my work term was completed. I read documentation, manuals and Scotia's main fraud page to ensure I was meeting all crietia and working as a reliable team member.

Goal 2, reading comprehension, was not only a challenge for the work term, but for myself as a person. I am able to recite long articles of text, and understand various reading voices, tones and vocabulary. But despite being able to comprehend writing, why would this be a goal at all? The effort of focus required to remain engaged and have a working memory of what was just explained is why this goal is a challenge. By having ADHD, reading for prolonged periods of time can get stressful quickly. I took action by splitting each task by catogory and read based on each category. This allowed me the opportunity to alternate between tasks before feeling drained, and also helped me connect ideas across tasks - helping my creativity and understanding. All in all, I was able to complete tedious tasks that required lots of reading in a way that not only meant success for the outcome, but also success for my own mental state and endurance.

This last goal, Goal 3, under technological literacy, adds onto goal 2 in that the work that comes out of my learned skills will benefit the teams the schedule gets busier and busier. A single document was created by my 2 coworkers and I to help new interns and Fraud employees get a grasp of the vast amount of information requried to complete work. This organization of details also helped me along the way with analyzing data and presenting ideas to stakeholders. As well as this, utilizing my preexisting skills helped me complete work at a fast pace, and this also helped the team stay on track of their busy schedules.

Work Term 1 | S21

Animal Biosciences - Shackle Turkey Imaging

First work term, but what is the role? The position itself involved precise photo editing skills, a strong attention to detail, and profound patience. Initially, the role sounds dull and repetative, unlike it's impact on the final product. Shackle Turkey Imaging consisted of a series of videos shackle turkeys after slaughter. Some of these turkeys has zip ties. These zipties signified a greater solution; to use thechnology to accurately measure the quality and weight of meat.

I had experience in photo editing using Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator and in drawing programs such as FireAlpaca and Clip Studio Paint. Using these skills, I was able to use VLC Media Player to capure .tiff snapshots of the passing turkeys, and remove the backgrounds using a photo editing program. Using Clip Studio Paint proved to be the most efficient method for this as keybinds were easy to implement, and the responsiveness of the application saved even more time. CSP and Photoshop's results were extreamly similar, and the results produced were identical. Since my primary computing device is my laptop (a Macbook), CSP was not only speedy but was also accessible from anywhere I could bring my laptop! That meant if the opportunity presented itself, I could work anywhere, not just in a home office.

Out of these 5 goals, the main improvements I wanted to make were communicating, creativity and personal growth. I believe I've achieved these goals over my work term, and will continue to improve as I succeed in other work terms. Through this work term, I've learned how much my environment can impact my work quality and speed.

Considering this work was to be completed on a computer, finding times to take breaks and a schedule that suits my ability to deliver my best work were one of my initial challenges. Hence my first goal; to find new ways of completing work. I became condifent in my ability to make my work as efficient as possible by taking advantage of the fact that this work was completed on a computer. Using technology allowed for my to be able to create shortcuts and organize all my work instantly using keybinds.

Working on my amplified shyness was another goal of mine; under the communication theme. I found that over the course of quaratine, my shyness had gotten worse. By trying to ensure I was communicating well while working, it would force me to go out of my comfort zone and, in turn, be a better employee as the work done would meet every requirement.

All in all, I feel as though I was able to complete my goals with the ability to continue with these goals for my next position. I was able to fulfill each goal adequately. I was able to communicate with my boss and co-worker, but can still work on my deliveries. My boss for this positon, Ryley, evaluated me on this with the tip that I should not apologise for asking many questions, and seeking clarification.
